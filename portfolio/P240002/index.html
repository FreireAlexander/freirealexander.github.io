OOCAD: Object-Oriented CAD Design

OOCAD is an innovative system that applies object-oriented programming principles and software design patterns to CAD projects. Its main goal is to enhance team productivity, streamline version control, and modularize components to avoid design repetition, making projects easier to modify and maintain.

The name OOCAD stands for "Object-Oriented Computer-Aided Design". Currently, the system's concept and logic are in development, with an initial focus on AutoCAD and similar applications.

A key principle of OOCAD is the creation of unique project components that evolve as client needs or project conditions change, allowing for continuous and efficient adaptation.
